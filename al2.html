Days by the Siruvani

■Enjoy a serene stay at our sprawling 3-acre resort, on the banks of River Siruvani – with the sweetest water in India, in one of our 33 chalets made entirely out of pinewood.

■Taj Anaikatti is on the banks of River Siruvani with the sweetest water in India. With 33 pinewood chalets in a rustic setting, surrounded by coconut trees, the resort has a swimming pool, spa and a host of indoor & outdoor games for a fun holiday.

■ The vast area of the resort has a kids play area and a swimming pool for guests to relax. The resort also has a Holiday Activity Centre for guests to enjoy indoor and outdoor games.





Resort highlights

◆Premium rooms offering up to 550 sq.ft of accommodation space with superior facilities for the family. Enjoy VR games, board games, paintball, archery and more.

◆Experience authentic Kerala cuisine and popular Indian and International favourites at our Planter’s Kitchen restaurant.

◆Relax and indulge in authentic ayurvedic treatments at the Subuthi spa.

◆Indulge in nature walks to appreciate a variety of flora and do birdwatching within the resort.
