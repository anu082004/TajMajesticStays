For the Nature Lover, Birdwatcher, Trekker and Traveller

■Enjoy a relaxing holiday in this 14.2-acre resort with abundant greenery, overlooking the Chinnakanal Lake and offering breathtaking views from your window.

■Taj Munnar is located on tea-covered slopes overlooking the Chinnakanal Lake. The 14.2-acre resort is the home for various exotic birds. The resort boasts of a spa & a multi-cuisine restaurant, several indoor & outdoor games within the resort.

■ The resort has a kids play area with swings, ropeways and slides that children can enjoy.

■It also has a Holiday Activity Centre for those who want to try their hands at table tennis, badminton, cricket or even board games.

■The resort also arranges bonfires, karaoke nights, party games and much more. Sumptuous meals, caring staff and the soothing atmosphere of the resort will make your stay at Taj Munnar memorable.



Resort highlights

◆Premium rooms offering up to 550 sq.ft of accommodation space with superior facilities for the family. Enjoy VR games, board games, paintball, archery and more.

◆Experience authentic Kerala cuisine and popular Indian and International favourites at our Planter’s Kitchen restaurant.

◆Relax and indulge in authentic ayurvedic treatments at the Subuthi spa.

◆Indulge in nature walks to appreciate a variety of flora and do birdwatching within the resort.

Dining


☑ Try local delicacies from Planter's Kitchen, the in-house restaurant at Taj Munnar.

☑ The restaurant offers buffet options and an a la carte menu to select from.
☑ Karimeen, the pearl spot fish is synonymous to Kerala.
